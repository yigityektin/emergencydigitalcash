EmergencyDigitalCash

RFID/NFC card → ENS subname → USDC payment (Sepolia)

What is this?

A tap-to-pay “emergency cash” card built with a cheap RC522 reader + STM32F103Tx. Each card UID deterministically maps to an EVM address. When tapped, the POS: (1) provisions an ENS subname under emergencycash-try.eth, (2) sets resolver records (addr + text), (3) pays the merchant in USDC on Sepolia.

How it works

  1. RC522 reads UID from the card
  2. POS derives card wallet address
  3. Creates/updates ENS: <uid>.emergencycash-try.eth
  4. Writes ENS records: addr, text(uid,type,token,chain,url)
  5. Sends PRICE USDC to the merchant

Demo checklist

  • ✅ Tap card → prints UID + derived address
  • ✅ ENS subname provisioned and records written
  • ✅ USDC payment sent to merchant
  • ✅ Lost-card safety: blocklist revoke/unrevoke
